Features

MagicGamepad is your ultimate solution for transforming your Android device into a versatile game controller. Designed with multiplayer gaming in mind, our app lets you and your friends join the fun seamlessly. Connect multiple devices and enjoy a collaborative and competitive gaming experience.

Game Compatibility

Most XInput-supported games are compatible with MagicGamepad, ensuring you can enjoy a wide range of popular games with ease

Volume Control

Adjust the volume directly using the volume buttons on your phone, providing a simple and intuitive way to manage audio levels while gaming or using your PC.

Multiplayer Support

Connect multiple devices to the same PC for an unparalleled multiplayer gaming experience. Play with friends and enjoy cooperative or competitive gameplay with ease

Keyboard and Touchpad Input

Type with ease using your Android device as a keyboard and navigate your PC effortlessly with the touchpad functionality. It is useful when you need to use a keyboard or mouse while gaming from a distance

How It Works

  1. Download and Install the Mobile App: Download MagicGamepad from the Google Play Store and install it on your Android device.
  2. Download and Install the Desktop App: Download the MagicGamepad desktop app for Windows from our website and install it on your PC.
  3. Connect Your Devices: Connect your Android device to your PC using Wi-Fi, USB tethering, or hotspot. Follow our easy setup guide to get started quickly.
  4. Select Your Controller Number: Choose your player number to ensure seamless multiplayer gaming. You can connect up to 4 devices for an engaging multiplayer experience.
  5. Enjoy: Start gaming, controlling media, or navigating your PC with ease using MagicGamepad.
